Dunia, Our Biracial Staff

Meet our Arab and Aussie biracial friend! She is also a staff of the Monsoon Review. Her name directly translates to "World" in English. Being kind and thoughtful towards everyone she meets, she strives to build a better reality for all. She prioritises others' feelings over her own. She is the moon of the group. A quiet person in real life, her mind constantly filled with thoughts and goals. She particularly enjoys things that allow her to spend a long time pondering upon. A true enthusiast of philosophy, she will never get enough of it. She is in charge of Arabic entries (Do include an English translation, though! Growing up in an English-speaking country, she is not very fluent in Arabic.) She hopes to be a safe place for all. She adores the act of kindness and treat people the way she would want to be treated, rather than treating them depending on whether they deserve it or not. Among her friends, she is often labelled as the peacemaker. She is true to people, and one need not be afraid to approach her. She promotes fairness, honesty, and loyalty. Such soul is difficult to encounter, Dunia is an irreplacable and priceless being.
